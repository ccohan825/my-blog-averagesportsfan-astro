This is the first article running through the new Average Sports Fan publishing system.

## Why the site is changing

The new website will make it much easier to write, edit, and publish football articles.

Instead of copying an entire HTML page, every article will be stored as a simple Markdown file.

## What comes next

The finished site will include:

- NFL articles
- College football articles
- Automatic category pages
- Automatic sitemap generation
- Image uploads
- A phone-friendly publishing dashboard
